ABOUT

Clinsignatures is a premium footwear care service and product Company, founded in 2018 by Zac David in Nigeria.

Our brand has been built through a grassroots approach. Our services provided by our Footwear Xperts ensure prolong life of your footwear, giving a clean look every time. Our process and products are specifically formulated to maintain and enhance the quality of your footwear. Be rest assured, your footwear will last longer in our care.

We are committed to high standards of professional conduct, thereby protecting the interests of customers and safeguarding the right of the public. Our Service will ensure your footwear are professionally cleaned. Your footwear will receive a thorough individual appraisal, complete with a full de-linting service.

It is our responsibility to ensure you keep clean footwear to prevent the effect of harmful bacteria and viruses in your home.

Stay Clean, Stay Healthy! Zac David (CEO)
